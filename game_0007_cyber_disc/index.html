Cyber Disc

Step onto the neon sports grid. Throw glowing cyber-discs to smash panels and moving drone targets on the back wall before the timer expires.

Static Grid Panels (100 Pts)

Standard neon wall targets. Clear them to trigger new waves.

Flying Drones (300 Pts)

Fast-moving targets sliding horizontally along the back wall.

Green Core (Explosive)

Hazard Node (-200 Pts)

Red warning target. Hitting it deducts points and freezes the dispenser.

VR Controls: Squeeze trigger/grip near dispenser to grab a disc. Swing arm and release to throw with full velocity.

Desktop Controls: Click dispenser to load. Click and hold to charge throw power, then release to launch towards the cursor.
