================================================================================
GAME DESIGN SKETCH: COSMIC DINER (0011)
================================================================================

1. HIGH CONCEPT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A fast-paced diner arcade game. The player is a bartender in a space station bar. 
Alien customers sit across the counter and order neon mixed drinks. The player must 
grab ingredients (Red, Green, Blue taps), mix them to create the requested colors, 
and slide the drinks to the matching customer before they leave angry.

2. HARDWARE CONSTRAINTS & ACCESSIBILITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Controls: WebXR controller laser pointer (aim, click trigger to pick up cups, click 
  taps to pour, click customers to serve). Desktop fallback uses standard mouse 
  clicks. No pointer lock.
* Field of View (FoV): The bar, ingredients, and customers are arranged in a 
  80-degree field of view to keep everything within arm's reach and view.
* Comfort: Stationary, standing behind the bar counter. The bar structure provides 
  a strong local horizontal plane of reference.

3. VISUAL LAYOUT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Diner: Retro-futuristic diner with glowing chrome counters and a starry window.
* Dispenser Station: Three vertical tube taps containing Red, Green, and Blue neon liquids.
* Customers: Floating alien entities positioned at three stools across the counter.
* Order Bubbles: Holographic colored bubbles floating above customer heads.

4. CORE GAMEPLAY LOOP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Start: Player clicks a glowing "OPEN BAR" sign.
2. Orders: Customers arrive and request a color (e.g., Purple, Cyan, Yellow, White).
3. Mixing: 
   - Player clicks a cup to spawn it on the mixing pad.
   - Player clicks a color tap. Liquid fills the cup and changes its color state:
     - Red + Blue = Purple
     - Green + Blue = Cyan
     - Red + Green = Yellow
     - Red + Green + Blue = White
4. Serving: Player clicks the completed cup, then clicks the customer who ordered it 
  to slide it across the bar.
5. Feedback: Serves reward cash (points) and add patience time. Wrong orders or 
  excessive waits result in customer departure and a loss of life.
6. Game Over: Occurs when 3 customers leave unsatisfied. Display cash earned.
