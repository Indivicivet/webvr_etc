================================================================================
GAME DESIGN SKETCH: NEON PUNCH (0008)
================================================================================

1. HIGH CONCEPT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A rhythm-action arcade game where the player stands at the end of a glowing runway 
and uses physical punching motions to shatter oncoming neon orbs. Orbs are color-coded 
(pink/blue) and must be hit by the corresponding hand (left/right) in sync with the beat.

2. HARDWARE CONSTRAINTS & ACCESSIBILITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Controls: WebXR controller position tracking (physical punch velocity/collision). 
  Desktop fallback uses mouse movements to position gloves and clicks (left-click 
  for left glove, right-click/space for right glove) or arrow keys to strike. No mouse lock.
* Field of View (FoV): All orb tracks are situated within a 50-degree front cone, 
  ensuring they are visible without neck strain.
* Comfort: Stationary, standing or seated. The track runway provides a strong sense 
  of depth and perspective, preventing visual disorientation.

3. VISUAL LAYOUT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Arena: A dark synthwave tunnel with neon grid lines running towards the player.
* Gloves: Translucent glowing spheres attached to the player's hand positions 
  (pink/magenta on the left, electric blue on the right).
* Target Orbs: Glowing spheres carrying core particles, emerging from the far end (z = -15m).
* Hit Zone: A neon white gate overlay at z = -1.2m where punches are evaluated.

4. CORE GAMEPLAY LOOP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Start: Player clicks a large "LAUNCH BEATS" button. Upbeat synthesized music starts.
2. Spawning: Orbs spawn at z = -15m on either the left or right runway lanes, 
  timed to match the music track's beats.
3. Punching: 
   - VR: The player punches when the orb reaches the Hit Zone. The game registers 
     a hit if the glove sphere intersects the orb.
   - Desktop: Player moves the mouse to aim and clicks the mouse keys/presses 
     keys to strike the corresponding lane at the right time.
4. Feedback: Shattering an orb plays a snare/clap sound and a burst of matching 
  colored sparks. Misses cause a dull synth thud and a shield gauge decrease.
5. Game Over: Occurs when shields drain. Display total hits, accuracy, and a retry button.
