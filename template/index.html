WEBXR

WebXR Playground

A premium, zero-build virtual reality template. Perfect for quick deployment on GitHub Pages.

🎮 VR Mode

  • Enter VR: Click the "ENTER VR" button at the bottom of the screen.
  • Aim: Point your VR controllers at the interactive shapes.
  • Select: Pull the Trigger to change colors & trigger haptics.

💻 Desktop / Fallback

  • Look Around: Drag with left mouse button / swipe.
  • Interact: Click on objects to trigger animations.
  • Pan/Zoom: Right-click drag / pinch to zoom.
